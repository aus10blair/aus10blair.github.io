Prison Break

Prison Break has gone through many stages of development. I worked alongside a colleague, Owusu Bonsu, to develop this game.
Prison Break first started out as a text-based game about navigating to find items that would aid in users' the escape from a Russian Gulag.
Initially, this game was a great resource to host our knowledge and skills of web development through html, css, and javascript.
As our knowledge and skills began to improve, the game could follow suit. The idea of the text-based game soon transitioned into a Unity engine
developed game. Many aspects of the game were changed the new development, but this transition allowed for experience with Unity game development.
Through the years of Prison Break, my personal fascination with front-end development came in handy. My knowledge of design and front-end development
were useful when developing the game's layout and characters, providing a better grasp of the game's setting for the user.
