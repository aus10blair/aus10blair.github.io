Prison Break

This game has gone through many stages of development. I worked alongside a colleague, Owusu Bonsu, to develop this game.
Prison Break first started out as a text-based game about navigating to find items that would aid in the escape of a Russian Gulag.
Initially this game was a great resource to host our knowledge of web development and skills such as html, css, and javascript.
As the knowledge and skills began to improve, the game could follow suit. The idea of the text-based game soon transitioned into a Unity engine
developed game. Many aspects of the game came along with the new development, but this transition allowed for new knowledge to be gained.
Through the years of Prison Break, my personal fascination with front-end development came into play. Developing this new Unity game needed aspects of design
both in layout and character that were based on the setting of the game.

The game can be found here: Prison Break.
